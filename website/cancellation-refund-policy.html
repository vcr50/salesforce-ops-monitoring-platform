Cancellation and Refund Policy

This policy applies to TomCodeX digital subscriptions and software services, including SentinelFlow.

Cancellation

You may cancel a monthly subscription at any time from the billing portal or by contacting support. Cancellation stops future renewals. Access normally remains available until the end of the current paid billing period unless otherwise required by law or your agreement.

Refunds

Subscription fees already paid are generally non-refundable once digital service access has been provisioned. If you believe you were charged in error, contact us within 7 days of the charge and we will review the request in good faith.

Failed or Duplicate Payments

Verified duplicate payments or accidental overcharges will be refunded to the original payment method after reconciliation with the payment gateway.

Enterprise Agreements

Enterprise plans may have separate cancellation, refund, service credit, or termination terms in the signed order form or master agreement. Those written terms will control where they differ from this policy.

Support

For cancellation or refund questions, contact support@tomcodex.com or use our Contact page.
