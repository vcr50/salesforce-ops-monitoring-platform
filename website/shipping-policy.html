Shipping Policy

TomCodeX provides software-as-a-service and digital products, including SentinelFlow. We do not ship physical goods for standard online subscriptions.

Digital Delivery

After successful payment and account verification, access to paid features is delivered digitally through the SentinelFlow application, Salesforce package, customer portal, or onboarding instructions shared by email.

Delivery Timeline

Most subscription access is provisioned automatically after payment confirmation. In some cases, activation may take up to 24 business hours if manual account, organisation, or Salesforce package verification is required.

No Physical Shipping Charges

Because SentinelFlow is a digital service, no shipping, handling, courier, or physical delivery charges apply unless a custom enterprise agreement explicitly states otherwise.

Support

For delivery or access questions, contact support@tomcodex.com or use our Contact page.
