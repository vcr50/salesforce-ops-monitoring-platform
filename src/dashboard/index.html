PRODUCT PITCH · Q&A
Q: What did you build?
SentinelFlow is an incident intelligence platform that helps operations teams detect, analyze, and resolve system failures — with full business impact visibility. Built natively on Salesforce, it combines real-time monitoring, AI-driven root cause analysis, and autonomous self-healing.
Q: Why is it different?
Traditional monitoring tools alert you that something broke. SentinelFlow tells you why it broke, what to do, and how much revenue is at risk — all in one unified view. The AI Copilot turns passive alerts into actionable, confident decisions.
Q: Why Salesforce?
Salesforce provides enterprise-grade infrastructure for data, automation (Flows, Apex), and real-time events (Platform Events). Agentforce unlocks native AI without third-party LLM dependencies. This lets us focus on product behavior rather than infrastructure plumbing.
